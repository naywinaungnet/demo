About Dr. Nay

Dr. Nay is a board-certified Family Medicine physician with over 15 years of experience dedicated to providing patient-centered care. She graduated from the prestigious Stanford University School of Medicine and completed her residency at Mayo Clinic. Dr. Nay believes in a holistic approach to medicine, focusing not just on treating illness but on promoting wellness and preventing disease.

Professional Portfolio

  • Board Certification: American Board of Family Medicine
  • Medical Degree: M.D., Stanford University School of Medicine
  • Residency: Family Medicine, Mayo Clinic, Rochester, MN
  • Professional Memberships: American Academy of Family Physicians (AAFP), World Medical Association (WMA)
  • Areas of Expertise: Chronic Disease Management, Preventive Care, Pediatrics, and Geriatrics.

Advanced Diagnostic Services

Laboratory Services

  • Blood Tests: Complete Blood Count (CBC), Lipid Panel, Glucose, Thyroid Function Tests, etc.
  • Urinalysis: Routine and microscopic examinations.
  • Pathology Services: Biopsy analysis and tissue diagnostics.
  • Microbiology: Culture and sensitivity testing for infections.
  • Allergy Testing: Comprehensive panels to identify allergens.

Radioimaging Services

  • X-ray: Digital X-rays for bone and chest imaging.
  • Ultrasound: Diagnostic imaging for various internal organs, including abdominal, pelvic, and prenatal ultrasounds.
  • DEXA Scan: Bone density scanning for osteoporosis detection.
  • Echocardiography: Ultrasound of the heart to assess its structure and function.
